How efficient was each stroke? (SEI chart)

What you’re looking at: This chart shows stroke efficiency (SEI) from the first stroke to the last. SEI is “how much distance you get per unit of effort” — like miles per gallon for your stroke. Higher line = more efficient. If the line goes down over time, the swimmer became less efficient (e.g. tired or form dropped). A red marker means we detected a clear drop in efficiency at that point in the swim.
